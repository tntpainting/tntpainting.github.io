Experience
TNT Painting has successfully restored and painted over 2000 homes and buildings in the past two decades. Our solid reputation for quality and continuing success reflect our philosophy. We believe that every job done well is the most effective advertising possible. We are a service oriented company, and our long list of satisfied customers will prove this.

Competitive Pricing
People are amazed that TNT Painting can offer such quality work at such competitive prices. How do we do it? We do it by organizing each job for maximum efficiency, by training each man to perform his tasks in the right order, and by providing the best tools and products obtainable. By estimating the cost of each job accurately, we insure long-term success. We save time... and that saves you money.

Neatness and Efficiency
In the painting business, neatness counts a lot. Our foremen carry a full complement of tarps, plastic sheeting, taping machines and other material and devices that may be required. This insures that paint goes where it's supposed to go. Driveways, walkways, patios, shrubbery, plants, windows, etc. are left neat and clean upon completion.

Quality Products
Thorough surface preparation and top quality paints help insure a lasting job. We only use premimum Dunn Edwards, Kelly-Moore and Benjamin Moore top line finishes. Our volume discounts allow us to provide these products at an affordable price.

Dependability
For over twenty years, we have been serving The North Bay and beyond. Every job is guaranteed in writing and, more importantly, we will be around to back it up. Our guarantees are not loaded with disclaimers that render it worthless. All problems which may arise are handled in a non-argumentative manner. Our reputation is our most important asset!

TNT Equals Stability

It is important to look beneath the surface of any company you hire to perform a painting or restoration project. Are they knowledgeable and stable? Or do they resemble the damaged stucco wall you are hiring them to repair? TNT Painting is very proud of our projects, our clients and our business success. We have company owned equipment to complete any size job, including high work and difficult access homes from heavy exterior coatings to fine interior finishes.
